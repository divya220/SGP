Plants Online - Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

1. Why are my houseplants getting brown tips on their leaves?

It happens when houseplants are exposed to too much fertilizer, and too many salts build up in the soil. And poor watering habits also lead to brown tips on leaves. Counter the problem with solutions. It is recommended to remove brown leaf tips and edges by using scissors or pruning shears.

2. Why is my plant not growing? How do I boost my plants growth?

There can be many reasons for your plant not growing, such as too much watering, or low watering, use of wrong fertilizer, and not providing the proper sunlight. Quick Solutions:

-Do a soil test to know its content

-Use the right type of fertilizers

-Talk to the plants

-Provide your plant with requires sunlight

3. Why is my plant not flowering?

It can be the cause of nutrient imbalance. Too much nitrogen can reduce flowering. Too little amount of phosphorus can also reduce flowering. Pruning too plays an important part and if you do not prune your plant appropriately, you may not get to see new flowering as they are expected.
